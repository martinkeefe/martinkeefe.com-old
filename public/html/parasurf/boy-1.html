Boy's Surface (1)

$$\begin{align} x& = \frac{2}{3} \frac{(\cos u \cos2v + \sqrt2 \sin u \cos v) \cos u}{\sqrt2 - \sin2u\sin3v}& u \in [0,\pi)\\ y& = \frac{2}{3} \frac{(\cos u \sin2v - \sqrt2 \sin u \sin v) \cos u}{\sqrt2 - \sin2u\sin3v}& v \in [0,\pi)\\ z& = \frac{\sqrt2\cos^2 u}{\sqrt2 - \sin2u \sin3v} \end{align}$$
