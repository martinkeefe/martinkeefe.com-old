Pub Watch

Keeping an eye on the slow extinction of the British pub. Here is a map of public watering holes, past and present, in Dorking and the surrounding area.

Alive Currently open.
In Limbo Closed but not converted to another use. Might reopen but on danger list.
Dead Closed and changed use in last decade or so. Could conceivably be resurrected.
Gone Demolished and/or converted to residential use. Gone forever.
Buried Disappeared at least 40 years ago.

Basic data is from CAMRA's WhatPub site. Some use also made of information from The Lost Pubs Project and UK Pub history.
